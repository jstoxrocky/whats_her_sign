Logging into Tinder

Step 1

Right click on the link below and select 'Copy Link Address'.

Link

Step 2

Open a new tab in your browser.

Step 3

Open up the Developers Console in this new tab. (Option+Cmd+J on a Mac with Google Chrome).

Step 4

Click the 'Network' tab in the Developers Console.

Step 5

Paste the URL you copied in Step 1 into the address bar and hit Enter.

Step 6

Under the 'Name' column, click the row beginning with 'oauth?client_id...'

Step 7

Under 'Response Headers', look for 'location'. Copy and paste the Access Token, beginning from 'access_token=' up to but not including '&expires_in=...'

Step 8

Paste this access token into the field below and Login.
